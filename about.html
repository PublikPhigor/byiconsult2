About Us

Our Vision

To ensure the provision of high-quality consultancy and ICT services for our customers, supporting them to achieve their strategic and business goals and ensuring continuity throughout the changes to their business.

Our Mission

To be recognized as a trusted provider of quality IT services and business solutions by helping businesses across sub-Saharan Africa stay one step ahead by transforming their networking and communication operations into competitive assets.

Our Core Values

Superior quality

Honesty

Boldness

Passion

BYI Consult is a leading independent ICT and consultancy company, specializing in business technology, consultancy, design and development of complex information systems, in the public and private sectors. We focus on business objectives and business needs, ensuring that each project delivers the right solutions to meet customers’ business goals and strategic targets. By using our extensive knowledge and experience, in collaboration with our customers, we provide customized solutions that are innovative and sustainable, to enable our customers to achieve business excellence and to deliver maximum value to the business.
